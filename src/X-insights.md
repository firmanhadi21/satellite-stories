# X Growth Insights — @jalmiburung

## For future Claude

Data-driven analysis of Firman Hadi's X account (@jalmiburung) built from his own exported analytics (Apr–Jun 2026), to grow impressions. The verdict: his highest-reach formula is **video + a stakes-first hook + his "ngedumel" personal-story voice**, tied to remote-sensing/policy so follows are relevant. Football replies bring empty reach; long numbered threads die after tweet 1. Use this to steer all X content drafting and the [[X-content-plan]]. Numbers are `confidence: high` (his own analytics CSVs in `Tweets/`).

## The numbers
- Daily impressions grew from ~200–900/day (early May 2026) to **10,000–17,000/day** (late June 2026) — driven by consistent daily posting. `confidence: high`
- Single best post: **17,797 impressions** — "Pesimis" traffic/klakson personal story **with video** (2026-06-23): 340 engagements, 217 detail-expands, 36 profile visits, 16 new follows.
- Best bookmark magnet: the **video "tahapan" explainer** (3,038 impressions, 40 bookmarks, 2026-06-27).
- Best RS reach: **mahasiswa wisuda / deteksi kumbang sawit thread starter** (1,449 impressions, 174 engagements, 2026-06-14).

## What works (double down)
1. **Video** — top 3 posts by reach all had video. Even simple Remotion/Claude explainers earn reach + bookmarks. `confidence: high`
2. **Personal "ngedumel" stories** (title line + narrative, his blog voice): traffic behaviour, sensus/BPS, "kata ganti Anda vs Saudara". His viral lane; drives profile visits & follows.
3. **Stakes-first hooks on RS content** — lead with money/policy/national stakes ("500 kota", "sangat mahal", "menunggu peta dari negara lain"), THEN the method.
4. **Bookmark-bait RS explainers** — R sits, U-Net, super-resolution. The engaged niche audience saves these → relevant follows.
5. **Consistency** — June's daily cadence is what moved the curve. `confidence: high`

## What to stop / fix
1. **Football replies** — big impressions, ~0 engagement, no relevant follows. Empty reach; deprioritize (or only when genuinely funny). `confidence: high`
2. **Long numbered threads (1/8…8/8)** — tweet 1 gets reach, the rest get 5–32 impressions. Go ≤4 tweets, each standalone, or convert to one atomic bookmark-bait tweet.
3. **Method-first hooks** — technical opening lines underperform. Reframe with human stakes.
4. **Links in body** — put links in the first reply, not the tweet body (X penalizes link-in-body reach).

## Reply strategy (convert reach to the right audience)
Reply with real takes to RS/GIS/tech/education/policy accounts, NOT football. Targets to curate: BRIN/BMKG/BIG, KLHK, data-journalism, urban-planning/tata-ruang, Indonesian academic & "dosen" community, AI/dev Indonesia.
