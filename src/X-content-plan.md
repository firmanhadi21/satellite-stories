# X Content Plan — 2 weeks (2026-06-29 .. 2026-07-12)

## For future Claude

A sustainable 2-week posting calendar for @jalmiburung, built from [[X-insights]]. Designed to grow impressions WITHOUT derailing the [[Traffic Analyses]] TRIP paper revision due 2026-07-14 — so it's light (1 main post/day + a short reply sprint). Format rotation favours his proven winners: video, stakes-first RS, personal ngedumel stories. Drafts live in [[X-swipe-and-drafts]]. Post times in WIB; avoid links in tweet body.

## Operating rules
- **1 main post/day** + **5–10 thoughtful replies** to RS/tech/education/policy accounts (the reply sprint is where reach compounds).
- Best times (WIB): **07:00, 12:00, 20:00**. Post the main piece at one of these; do replies opportunistically.
- Video > thread > atomic > reply (in reach). Aim for **2 videos/week**.
- Threads ≤ 4 tweets, each standalone. Links go in the first reply.
- Engage for ~20 min after posting (early replies drive the algorithm).
